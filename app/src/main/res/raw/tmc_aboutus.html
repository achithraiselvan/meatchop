About Us

The Meatchop is the go-to-place for all meats and other meat supplementary products. We strive on the vision that ‘Food tastes great when cooked at home’. We, the MeatChop aim at encouraging you to tap the inner chef in yourself to prefer home-cooked food always. The brand offers your favorite pasture-raised meat and seafood in your desired cuts that are free from antibiotics and hormones, delivered to your kitchen. Our chefs' team prepares the fresh ingredients and provides you with bundle offerings ensuring that you never need to indulge in any recipe hunting or ingredients shopping or tedious preparation. We also enable you to cook like a wizard by offering step by step recipes along with our products. The brand intends to provide an enriched experience by simplifying the entire meat shopping process along with the food preparation, allowing you to enjoy your mealtime at home to the fullest.
